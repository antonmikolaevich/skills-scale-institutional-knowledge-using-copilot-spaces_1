# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

## Overview

OctoAcme employs a comprehensive project management methodology that encompasses the complete project lifecycle from initiation through retrospective and continuous improvement. Our approach integrates industry best practices with organizational requirements to ensure successful project delivery.

## Core Project Management Principles

- **Customer-first**: Prioritize customer value and usability in all project decisions.
- **Iterative delivery**: Deliver small, testable increments to accelerate feedback and reduce risk.
- **Clear ownership**: Each project has a named Project Manager (PM) and Product Lead responsible for outcomes.
- **Data-informed decisions**: Measure impact and iterate based on evidence and metrics.
- **Psychological safety**: Encourage feedback, learning, and continuous improvement.

## Core Project Management Process

Our process is structured across six key phases:

1. **Initiation** - Validate business need, align stakeholders, and authorize project work
2. **Planning** - Develop detailed project plans, define scope, and create actionable backlogs
3. **Execution & Tracking** - Execute tasks, monitor progress, and manage day-to-day delivery
4. **Risk & Communication** - Identify and manage risks, maintain transparent stakeholder communication
5. **Release & Deployment** - Deploy solutions to production safely and verify success
6. **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement** - Reflect on outcomes and enhance processes

## Core Roles

- **Project Manager (PM)**: Coordinates delivery, manages schedules, risks, and communications
- **Product Manager (PdM)**: Defines outcomes, prioritizes the backlog, and measures success
- **Developers**: Design, build, test, and deliver software components
- **QA/Testing**: Validate quality and ensure acceptance criteria are met
- **Stakeholders**: Provide inputs, approvals, and strategic direction

## Key Artifacts

Throughout the project lifecycle, OctoAcme teams create and maintain:

- **Project Charter / One-pager** - High-level project definition and business case
- **Roadmap and Release Plan** - Strategic timeline and delivery milestones
- **Sprint/Iteration Backlog** - Prioritized work items with acceptance criteria
- **Risk Register** - Tracked risks, impact assessment, and mitigation plans
- **Retrospective Notes** - Learnings and action items for continuous improvement
- **Release Notes** - Changes, known issues, and migration guidance

## Communication Cadence

- **Daily standups** (15 min) - Team syncs on progress, blockers, and dependencies
- **Weekly PM sync** - Alignment between Project Manager and Product Manager
- **Twice-weekly delivery standups** - Execution team synchronization (or as agreed)
- **Monthly stakeholder updates** - Executive and sponsor briefings
- **Sprint/Milestone reviews** - Demo and feedback from stakeholders
- **Ad-hoc escalations** - As-needed communication for critical issues
